Did you know that:
Earth's rotation is gradually slowing down, by about 17 milliseconds per century, making days slightly longer.
Saturn could float in water because it's mostly made of gas and has a lower density than water.
Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, which is three times the height of Mount Everest.
Neptune's extreme pressure turns carbon into diamonds, making it rain in its atmosphere.
